SHARKS

Great White Shark

The great white shark is one of the most well-known and studied species of chark. it is a large predator found in coastal surface waters in all major oceans.

Hammerhead Shark

Hammerhead sharks are easily recognized by their distinctive head shape. They inhabit warm waters around the world, often in coastal regions and continental shelves.
